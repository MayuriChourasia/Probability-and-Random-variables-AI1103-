\question If $\frac{2}{11}$ is the probabaility of an event, what is the probability of the event 'not A'.\\
\\
\solution \\
Let $A$ be the given event, then:\\
Probability of event $A$, that is, $\pr{A}= \frac{2}{11}$\\

\tofind Probability of not $A$, that is, probability of the event complimentary to $A$.\\
\\
Let $\pr{not A}=\pr{A'}$\\
we know that;
\begin{align}
    \pr{A}+\pr{A'}&=1\\
    \pr{A'}&=1-\pr{A}\\
    &=1-\brak{\frac{2}{11}}\\
    &=\brak{\frac{9}{11}}
\end{align}
\final The probability of the event 'not A' is $\brak{\frac{9}{11}}$
